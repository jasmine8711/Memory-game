Welcome to my food memory game. You have to click two cards one by one, if they are the same dish,they will stay on the table, if they are not the same dish ,they will be gone .And when you have all your food on the table, you win!!!

You can also use your arrow key on the keyboard to select the card, and press enter key to flip the card !

Also, if you are so sick of this game ,you can press "cheat" button to start the cheating version of the game .In the cheating version, you have 2s to remember all the food .

You can also change the back of the card to your photo!

Have fun!!!!!
